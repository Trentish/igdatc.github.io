Virtual Integration Center – a theater/conference room used for high profile meetings. It's large 8 foot by 23 foot immersive curved screen, along with its pixel management system, provide an impressive way to demonstrate system capability and design. Two demonstrations will be shown:
o Weapon of Mass Destruction scenario. A live land attack simulation of the Advanced Gun System on a DDG-1000, the US Navy’s future destroyer.
o AGS Operational Virtual Prototype. One of the specialties of the M&S team is functional demonstration of complex systems. This demonstration shows the inner workings of a large caliber gun system.
