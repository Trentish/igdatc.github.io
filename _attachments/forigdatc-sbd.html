Serious Game, Small Boats vs. Naval Destroyer

o Ship self defense demonstration environment. Used to showcase a number of BAE System products and systems, this demonstration pits player against player in a naval engagement. One side is the attackers where a couple players control small attack boats as the other side uses various weapon systems and tactics to defend themselves.
