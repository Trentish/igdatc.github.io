Serious Game, Infantry Fighting Vehicle

o Ground Combat Vehicle (GCV) interactive simulation. See BAE Systems proposed GCV in this interactive simulation using the VBS2 battlefield simulation engine. This multi-station setup allows players to take control of the vehicle driver, commander and gunner stations when they come under fire from hostile forces.
