This is the International Game Developer Association chapter for the Minneapolis/St Paul (Twin Cities) metro area in Minnesota, USA.  We are an active chapter and usually meet monthly and always welcome new visitors (with or without IGDA membership).

We invite you to return to our blog often, subscribe or follow us on Facebook and Twitter, for updates and event announcements
